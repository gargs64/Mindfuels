About Us

Welcome to Mind Fuels Publisher and Distributers. Based in North West Delhi, we are passionate creators of educational and nursery books designed to spark curiosity and fuel the potential in every child.

With a presence spanning over 20 years, we've become a trusted name among parents and teachers for our commitment to quality content and engaging learning experiences.

Shipping Policy

We strive to get our books into the hands of young readers as quickly as possible. Here is how we handle shipping:

  • Processing Time: All orders are processed within 48 business hours.
  • Delivery Timeline: Domestic shipping via reputed courier partners typically takes 3-7 business days depending on your location.
  • Tracking: Once your order is shipped, you will receive a tracking ID via email/SMS to monitor your delivery status.
  • Shipping Charges: Shipping fees are calculated at checkout based on the weight and destination.

Refund & Cancellation

Your satisfaction is our priority. If you encounter any issues with your purchase, please review our policy:

  • Return Window: You can request a return or refund within 7 days of delivery for defective, damaged, or incorrect items.
  • Cancellation: Orders can be cancelled before they are dispatched. Once shipped, the return process applies.
  • Refund Process: Approved refunds are processed to the original payment method within 10 working days.
  • Conditions: Books must be in their original condition and packaging to qualify for a return.

Privacy Policy

At Mind Fuels, we value your trust above all. We are committed to protecting your personal information.

Data Security & Payment

Explicit Privacy Commitment: Mind Fuels Publisher and Distributers does not store user passwords or sensitive credit card/banking information. All financial transactions are processed securely through Razorpay, our PCI-DSS compliant payment aggregator.

Information Collection

We collect basic information like name, email, and shipping address only to fulfill your orders and enhance your shopping experience. We never sell your data to third parties.
